Projects About

I am a senior at the Milwaukee School of Engineering University (MSOE), studying User Experience (UX) with a minor in Psychology. I am interested in UX research, looking to discover users wants and needs they did not even know they wanted in their products, as well as common pain points that they run into routinely. I do this through a variety of different methods like persona development, usability testing, surveys, and interviewing. I regularly put these skills to practice at my UX researcher internship at Johnson Controls as well as in the classroom at MSOE. I also work part-time as a Sales Associate at J.Crew Factory, where I enjoy implementing my previous training in textiles with new knowledge of user experience in a retail environment. Outside of my studies, I am a student-athlete playing on the Womens Soccer team at MSOE and I enjoy running, biking, and spending time with my pet hedgehog Sammy.
